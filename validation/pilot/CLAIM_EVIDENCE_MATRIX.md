# Pilot claim-to-evidence matrix

This matrix records the audit verdict for every proposed pilot headline. Public artifacts contain
sanitized evidence only; private source locations and raw records are intentionally omitted.

| Proposed claim | Verdict | Public evidence and check | Approved wording |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

The design treats fixtures as clusters and discloses purposive synthetic construction. | 30 distinct, self-contained controlled synthetic fixtures across six domains; not statistically independent samples. |
